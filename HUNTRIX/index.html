HUNTR/X is a three-member fictional project girl group. They were created for the Netflix animation film KPop Demon Hunters.

On April 24, 2025, Netflix unveiled a first look at the animated film K-Pop Demon Hunters and its K-pop trio, HUNTR/X, composed of Rumi, Mira, and Zoey—voiced respectively by Arden Cho, May Hong, and Ji-young Yoo. The superstar group sells out stadiums by day and secretly fights demons by night. The film follows their battle against their biggest threat yet: the Saja Boys, a rival boy band of demons in disguise whose goal is to destroy the hunters for good—and steal their fans.
